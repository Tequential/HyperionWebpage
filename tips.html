Why do cakes crack when baking?
  1. Too much baking powder - this causes the cake to rise too quickly and crack.
  2. Oven temperature is too hot - the crust is formed too early and the inside of the cake continues to rise.
Do I need to follow the recipe precisely? When cooking, feel free to experiment as long as you stick to the cooking time and temperature.
Baking is a science - every ingreident has a purpose so it is important to follow the recipe.
How important is salt when cooking? Salt is extremely important when cooking - it enhances the natural flavours in foods.
What is the difference between baking powder and baking soda? Both are leavening agents. Baking soda needs to be activated bu a liquid and an acid to help with rising. Baking powder is a complete leavening agent - it already contains the base (baking soda) and acid needed for the product to rise.
